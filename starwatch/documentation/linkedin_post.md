🛰️ I built STARWATCH — an open-source "security guard" for satellites.

Satellites are controlled by radio messages from Earth. Hackers have gotten
frighteningly close to abusing that: in 2008, intruders achieved every step
needed to command a NASA satellite, and in 2022 the Viasat attack knocked out
satellite internet across Europe one hour before the invasion of Ukraine.

STARWATCH is my proof-of-concept that simulates this problem and the defense:

🔍 It watches every (simulated) message sent to a fleet of satellites
🚨 Rule-based + AI anomaly detection catches fake commands, message floods, and unknown senders
📊 Each threat gets a danger score from 0–100
🤖 An AI layer explains every alert in plain English, with recommended actions

The best part: the whole demo runs in your browser. No install, no server.
You press a button to "become the hacker," then watch the system catch you.
There's even a live test console proving the detection logic isn't faked, and
a page documenting the real incidents each simulation is based on (with
sources).
