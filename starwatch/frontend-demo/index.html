🛰️ What is STARWATCH?

Satellites are controlled by radio messages sent from Earth. A hacker who fakes those messages could take over a satellite. STARWATCH is a security guard for satellites: it watches every incoming message, spots suspicious ones, gives each threat a danger score from 0–100, and uses AI to explain the problem in plain English. Everything here is a simulation — no real satellites involved, but the attacks are modeled on real incidents: see Real attacks & defenses.

How to use this demo

  1. Pick a satellite from the list on the left.
  2. Pretend to be the hacker: press one of the "Simulate an attack" buttons.
  3. Watch STARWATCH catch it — the danger gauge jumps and a red alert appears in the timeline.
  4. Click the alert to read the AI's explanation of what happened and what to do about it.

Your progress is saved automatically in this browser — close the tab and come back tomorrow, and you'll resume right where you stopped.
